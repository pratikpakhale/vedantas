37. Smriti also states this.
Smriti also declares that men not belonging to an  srama grow in knowledge through prayer and the like. 'Through prayer also a Br hmana may become perfect. May he perform other works or not, one who befriends all creatures is called a Br hmana' (Manu Smri. II, 17).
