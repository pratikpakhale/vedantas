8. Hence the awaking from that.
Since Brahman alone directly is the place of deep sleep, Scripture is able to declare that the souls awake from that, i.e. Brahman; compare 'Having come back from the True they do not know that they come from the True' (Kh. Up. VI, io, 2), and other texts.--Here terminates the adhikarana of 'the absence of that.'
