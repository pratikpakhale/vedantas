45. And on account of contradictions.
Moreover, manifold contradictions are met with in the Bh gavata system, with reference to the assumption of qualities and their bearers. Eminence of knowledge and

ruling capacity, strength, valour, and glory are enumerated as qualities, and then they are in some other place spoken of as Selfs, holy V sudevas, and so on.--Moreover, we meet with passages contradictory of the Veda. The following passage, for instance, blames the Veda, 'Not having found the highest bliss in the Vedas S ndilya. studied this s stra.'--For this reason also the Bh gavata doctrine cannot be accepted.
