39. Better than this is the other (state of belonging to an  srama), on account of the indicatory marks.
'Than this,' i.e. 'than standing between,' a better means of knowledge it is to stand within one of the  sramas, since this is confirmed by Sruti and Smriti. For scripture supplies an indicatory mark in the passage, 'On that path goes whoever knows Brahman and who has done holy
p. 317
works (as prescribed for the  sramas) and obtained splendour' (Bri. Up. IV, 4, 9); and Smriti in the passage, 'Let a Br hmana stay not one day even outside the  srama; having stayed outside for a year he goes to utter ruin.'
