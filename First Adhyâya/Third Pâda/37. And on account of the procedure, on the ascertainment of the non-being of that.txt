37. And on account of the procedure, on the ascertainment of the non-being of that.
That a S dra is not qualified for knowledge of Brahman appears from that fact also that as soon as Gautama has convinced himself that G b la, who wishes to become his pupil, is not a S dra, he proceeds to teach him the knowledge of Brahman.
